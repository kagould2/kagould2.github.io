Introduction

This website outlines some of the invasive species found in the upper Midwest region of the United States.

Types of Invasive Species

Asian Carp

These fish are known for their ability to quickly reproduce and dominate aquatic ecosystems, often outcompeting native species.

Emerald Ash Borer

An invasive beetle species that has devastated ash tree populations in the region, leading to widespread tree loss.

Zebra Mussels

Small freshwater mollusks that attach themselves to surfaces in lakes and rivers, causing damage to infrastructure and native species.

Impact

Invasive species can have significant ecological and economic impacts, including habitat destruction, loss of biodiversity, and increased management costs.
